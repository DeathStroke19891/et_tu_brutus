Elden Ring

Elden Ring cover art

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game was released in February 2022 and has been highly acclaimed for its intricate world design, deep lore, and challenging gameplay. Players embark on a journey through the Lands Between, a fantastical world filled with mythical creatures, epic bosses, and rich storytelling.

The game features an open-world design, allowing players to explore vast landscapes, uncover hidden secrets, and engage in dynamic combat. Elden Ring combines elements from previous FromSoftware titles such as Dark Souls, Bloodborne, and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, creating a unique and immersive experience.
